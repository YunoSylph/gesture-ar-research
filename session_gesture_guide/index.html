Gesture session - perform in this order

Object control task. Return your hand to neutral (open, relaxed) for ~2 seconds between each step.

Step 1 Point → pointer_hover Two-finger point at the object and hold briefly.
Step 2 Click → select_confirm Two-finger click (index + middle) to select.
Step 3 Zoom in → zoom_in Spread thumb and index apart (pinch open). Keep the hand still.
Step 4 Zoom out → zoom_out Bring thumb and index together (pinch close). Keep the hand still.

How to record: in the app pick Webcam + Robust C6 + Object control task, press Start Task, then do steps 1→2→3→4. Stop, and repeat for 3-5 separate sessions.

For clean numbers: sit facing a light source and keep your whole hand in frame at medium distance - on earlier logs half the frames lost the hand. Do one gesture at a time, pausing in neutral between them.

Note: steps 1-2 are real IPN reference clips; steps 3-4 are the thumb-index pinch the live controller actually uses (the dataset zoom clip moved the whole hand and no longer matches).
